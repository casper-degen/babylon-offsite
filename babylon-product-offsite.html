07 · Decision Making

Data-Driven Approach

Every decision backed by on-chain data and revenue forecasts.

📏
Revenue Forecasting
  • Every idea needs a revenue model before backlog entry
  • Format: IF X THEN TVL/borrow/fees change by Y BECAUSE Z
  • Post-ship: actuals vs. forecast review
🔍
On-chain Research
  • Track vault metrics, utilization trends, liquidation patterns
  • Monitor competitor protocols: integrations, pricing, UX changes
  • Validates hypotheses before building, saves sprint cycles
🔁
Experiment → Learn
  • Testnet as hypothesis validation environment
  • Post-launch reviews: forecast vs. reality
  • Learnings feed next RFC and cusdev cycle
Rule: can't describe revenue impact in one sentence with a number? Not ready for backlog.

09 · In Practice

Process in Action

Two-vault split UX. An unbuilt feature moving through the funnel.

💡
Idea
TWO-VAULT SPLIT UX Guide users to split BTC into sacrificial + protected vault at peg-in.
💰
Revenue Filter
Reduces cliff-effect risk → institutional confidence → larger positions → higher fees. On-chain comparison: full liquidation (AAVE) vs partial liquidation (Morpho) to validate revenue hypothesis. PASS
🔧
Capacity Filter
Protocol computes recommended split from on-chain THF + CF parameters and user position values. Dev estimate: only front end changes, no smart contract change. PASS
👥
Cusdev
Interview 5+ vault users: "How important is the split UX vs. current single-vault flow?" Score importance vs. effort.
